Step Three

Palming

This time it's only about darkness. Press Your closed eyes with Your palms. At first You'll see lots of this funny, fireworks-like dots, but relaxed eyes see nothing but pure black background.
Tip no. 1: don't press too hard, You shouldn't feel pain!

Step Four

The Final Battle.. with muscle tension

The Idea behind the stereographs is pretty easy. It's just like magic - completely frustrating and hard at the beginning, but pretty funny when You can do it.

If Your eyes are relaxed, instead of monotonous background You'll see some other picture, which feels like 3D. Now, there are few techniques to actually see the stereogram, but You have to find out yourself, which suites You the best - just try to relax and, by unfocusing eyesight, let everything get blurry.

From my experience, contact lenses make watching stereograms harder; I also usually put my glasses of, which helps as well.
